About Us

OpedTech is a startup tech support company specializing in PC performance enhancement as well as general IT support and cosmetic electronic mods. Founded by Samuel Smith, OpedTech was designed primarily to provide reliable and efficient information to those stumped with their tech issues. Check out what we offer Here!

Our Quest

Here at OpedTech we strive to provide the most reliable information to our customers at record speeds. While support from providers is often spotty at best, we hope to enhance your overall tech experience ten fold at a fraction of the cost.

Meet the Founder

Samuel Smith founded this company after being stumped on a problem of his own and noticing how much tech help is out there, but how little of it is truly reliable and helpful. He aims to create a tech support environment where users can easily seek quick, reliable, and cost effective information from our experts.
